The Binding of Isaac : Repentence

Recommended


Originally released in 2011 by Super Meat Boy developer Edmund McMillen, The Binding of Isaac is rightly considered a classic by fans of rogue-likes and many others. It is based on the Biblical story the same name, which has allowed for a fascinating direction in the games design. Mechanics, environments, characters and items all relate to this theme. The game has recieved multiple expansions and updates since the intial release with the most recent (and final) 'Repentence' releasing last year. Intruiging story, fantastic mechanics and potentially hundreds of hours of gameplay included.

  • Genre: Rogue-like, Procedural, Action-adventure
  • Gameplay Loop: Combat, character builds, bosses
  • Gameplay Time: 1 - 1000 hours
  • Pros: Rogue-like, interesting theme, endless builds, branching paths, hundreds of items, unlocks/challenges/achievements
  • Considerations: Addiction Warning!! If this game clicks with you, you are going to be in a relationship with it. Love AND hate will is innevitable.
